Why Do You Need a GEO-Optimized Bio?

AI is changing how clients find agents. When someone asks ChatGPT, Gemini, or Perplexity "Who's the best real estate agent in Kitchener for first-time buyers?" — the AI reads agent bios across the web and recommends whoever is the best match.

A regular bio won't cut it. A GEO-optimized bio (Generative Engine Optimized) is structured with the right keywords, specialties, and location signals so that AI search engines find you and recommend you over the competition.

Think of it like SEO, but for AI. The agents who update their bios now will be the ones AI recommends first. Use the Bio Builder below to create yours in under 5 minutes.
